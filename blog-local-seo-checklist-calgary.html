Local SEO checklist for Calgary service businesses

Follow this list to improve your visibility in local search and attract more qualified leads.

Article

12 quick wins for better local rankings

These steps are simple but high impact when done consistently.

1. Set up and verify your Google Business Profile.

2. Keep your business name, address, and phone consistent everywhere.

3. Add primary and secondary business categories.

4. Upload fresh photos each month.

5. Ask happy clients for keyword-rich reviews.

6. Add local service area keywords to your homepage and service sections.

7. Use unique title and description tags on every page.

8. Add structured data for your business and FAQ.

9. Build dedicated pages for top services.

10. Publish local helpful blog content regularly.

11. Ensure your site is mobile-friendly and fast.

12. Submit your sitemap in Google Search Console.
