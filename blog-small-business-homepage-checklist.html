Must-have sections for higher conversions

If a visitor lands on your homepage for 10 seconds, these sections should make your value obvious.

1. One clear headline that says who you help and what outcome you deliver.

2. A short supporting line with your location and main service.

3. A primary call-to-action button above the fold.

4. Trust proof: testimonials, reviews, or portfolio examples.

5. Simple service overview with pricing guidance.

6. Process section so clients know what happens next.

7. FAQ section to reduce objections.

8. Contact form with as few fields as possible.

9. Mobile-friendly layout and fast loading visuals.

10. Internal links to service pages and blog posts.
